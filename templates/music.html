Dillon Estrada is a touring drummer specializing in studio and live performances. He currently resides in the Austin, TX area and holds a degree from the well-renowned South Plains College Commercial Music program.

Dillon is currently on the road with long-time Texas Country favorite Kyle Park and has had the opportunity to work with artists such as:

  • Shotgun Rider
  • Randall King
  • Koe Wetzel
  • Dylan Wheeler
  • Kaitlin Butts
  • and many more.
